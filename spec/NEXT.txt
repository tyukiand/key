     a) Implement `menu_compile(ast) -> Vec<InteractiveInput>`

     b) Round-trip property: for any AST `A`,
        `interactive_engine(menu_compile(A)) == A`
        structurally.
     c) Run the property over four sources, per §B.8.5:
        (i) every ExampleControl in the verbose EDSL guide;
        (ii) every Control AST loadable from the in-tree
        fixture corpus tests/fixtures/**/*.yaml;
        (iii) proptest-generated random well-formed ASTs
        (1024 cases or proptest default; shrinks on
        failure). Generator MUST be FINITE AND BOUNDED:
        per-call `fuel: usize` decremented on recursion
        with a non-recursive-leaf branch at fuel 0;
        depth cap (default 8); list-width cap (default
        5) on every list-shaped variant; total node
        budget (default 200). Generator unit tests
